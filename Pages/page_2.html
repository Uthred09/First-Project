POSITIVE IMPACTS OF TECHNOLOGY

Technology has had a profound impact on the world, significantly changing the way we live our daily lives. Advancements in technology are continuously shaping various aspects of society such as:-

2.Healthcar
3.Governance
4.Education

Faster and more convenient communication through email, instant messaging and video conferencing. Real-time health monitoring through electronic health records, telemedicine, and wearable devices. Renewable energy technologies has reduced the negative impact on environment. New opportunities for learning through online courses, virtual classrooms, and educational apps.

NEGAVTIVE IMPACTS OF TECHNOLOGY

- With the rise of technology, people have become less reliant on face-to-face interactions for building relationships.

- Excessive use of technology, such as video games, has led to a decrease in physical activity and a decline in the health of young people.

- The implementation of automated systems in manufacturing companies has led to job loss for many employees.

-Thirdly, technology has made people lazy and unwilling to engage in strenuous activities....(Harrington, 2008, p.113)

On the other hand, There are many controversial medical technologies such as gene therapy, pharmacogenomics and stem cell research (Hunt, 2008, p.113). The use of genetic research in finding new cures for diseases is imperative and laudable, too in behave of benefits of technology...

CONCLUSION

Technology has become an integral part of modern life, providing a wide range of benefits that have made our lives easier and more comfortable. It has revolutionized communication, transportation, and other aspects of daily living, helping us to accomplish tasks more quickly and efficiently. Technologies such as vaccines have eradicated certain diseases and the internet has played an important role in promoting health and healthcare. Additionally, governments have leveraged technology to improve the delivery of services and enhance defense and security. However, technology also has its downsides, such as negative effects on human interactions and socialization. Overall, the key is to use technology responsibly, to reap its benefits while minimizing its downsides.
